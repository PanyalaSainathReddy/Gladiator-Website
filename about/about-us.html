Welcome to Gladiator Securitas & Allied Services Pvt. Ltd

Gladiator is a leading security and facilities firm, established since 2003 in Andhra Pradesh. Gladiator is a registered company under Register of Companies Act & Shops & Establishment Act having it's Registered Office in Visakhapatnam with Branch Offices in Hyderabad, Vijayawada, Rajahmundry, Kakinada & Tirupati.

Gladiator is managed and administered by core professionals having significant and proven experience in the field of Security & Facility Solutions. Gladiator with a strong manpower base over 2000 offering it's service's to varied market-segments including Power Plants, Port Sectors, Automobiles, Mines & Metals, Cement, Petrochemicals, Chemicals, Banks, Hotels, Hospitals, IT Sectors, Educational Institutions, Textiles, Clubs, Amusement Parks, Service Apartments, Theatres/ Multiplexes/ Malls, Manufacturing & Corporate Offices etc..

Gladiator expertise and reliable efforts in this field are rewarded by our Clients through our long lasting business relations with our esteemed Clients. Many of our Clients are associated with Gladiator for over 15 years. We are licensed under “PSARA Act 2005″ - The Private Security Agency Regulation Act.

MISSION

Gladiator mission is to provide service excellence to the Client's and deliver the highest quality standards with appropriate needs in Security Guarding & Facility Management Solutions.

VISION

Gladiator vision is to become one of the top league companies in Security & Facilities Industry across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana Regions.
