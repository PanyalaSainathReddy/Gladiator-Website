Welcome to Gladiator Securitas & Allied Services Pvt. Ltd

Gladiator Securitas is a leading security and facilities firm, established since 2003 in Andhra Pradesh & Telangana. Gladiator Securitas is a registered company under Register of Companies Act & Shops & Establishment Act having it's Registered Office in Visakhapatnam with Branch Offices in Hyderabad, Vijayawada, Rajahmundry, Kakinada & Tirupati.

Gladiator Securitas is managed and administered by core professionals having significant and proven experience in the field of Security & Facility Solutions. Gladiator Securitas with a strong manpower base over 3000+ offering it's service's to varied market-segments including Power Plants, Port Sectors, Automobiles, Mines & Metals, Cement, Petrochemicals, Chemicals, Banks, Hotels, Hospitals, IT Sectors, Educational Institutions, Textiles, Clubs, Amusement Parks, Service Apartments, Theatres/ Multiplexes/ Malls, Manufacturing & Corporate Offices etc..

Gladiator Securitas expertise and reliable efforts in this field are rewarded by our Clients through our long lasting business relations with our esteemed Clients. Many of our Clients are associated with Gladiator Securitas for over 15 years. We are licensed under “PSARA Act 2005″ - The Private Security Agency Regulation Act.

About Managing Director

Sri Jaladi Srinivasa Rao

Managing Director

Sri JALADI SRINIVASA RAO, after his Graduation Joined in C.R.P.F in 1987. He had undergone Physical Training (PT) course and National Security Guard (NSG) course meant exclusively for providing security cover to V.I.P's for Govt. of India Services

Assignment to VIP's (Govt. of India)

Soon after completion of NSG course he was posted as a group leader to provide security protection for Honourable Home Minister of Govt. of India, Sri Arjun Singh, he provided security cover throughout his activities while traveling across and all over India for two years. And the next assignment was to provide Security Officer for Late Mr. Beanth Singh who was then the Chief Minister of Punjab for 2 ½ years. Later he provided Security cover to the Honourable Health Minister of Punjab Mr. H.S. Bard who later became the Chief Minister of Punjab. He worked under him for 3 years long. After completion of the deputation, he was shifted to Manipur for combat operations of ULFAS in Imphal for 2 years.

After a successful career in defence with expertise knowledge in Security, Guarding & Surveillance operations, he started career with G4S, an MNC as a Sales Head and achieved desirable profits to the organization and as a result he was promoted as a Branch Head to cover entire Andhra Pradesh region. With proven experience to provide Security cover and Guarding various V.I.P's of Govt. of India and serving in defence with an added value experience of above 07 years in reputed MNC Security Companies enabled him to establish his own Security Firm in the market. And with the blessings and guidelines of his well-wishers Gladiator Securitas & Allied Services was formed in the market in 2003 and achieved tremendous success by having 3000+ Employee Base with 40 crore turnover.
